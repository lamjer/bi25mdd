Strong password creation tool for individuals

This project aims to explore and compare various methods of creating strong passwords and identify the most effective method. This project will also create a user friendly tool for password creation based on the identified method.! In today's digital age, where online threats are on the rise, it's crucial to protect your personal and sensitive information with strong and secure passwords. With this tool, you can ensure that your passwords meet the highest security standards and keep your accounts safe from unauthorized access.
